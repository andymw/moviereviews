\movie{Vertigo}{1958}{128m}{\fourplus} \dir{Alfred Hitchcock} \cast{James Stewart; Kim Novak; Barbara Bel Geddes; Tom Helmore} A retired detective suffering from acrophobia is enlisted to investigate the strange behavior of his old friend's wife, while developing a dangerous obsession with her. \textit{Vertigo} is Hitchcock's finest feature and one of the greatest films of all time. Tense and suspenseful throughout, with a shocking twist ending, everything about this film succeeds. Especially memorable is Hitchcock's master camerawork in the climactic tower sequence. \author{DW} \mpaa{PG, 1983}
